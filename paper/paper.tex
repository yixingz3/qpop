\begin{abstract}
% Port from paper/abstract.md.
We present a pre-registered, agent-assisted research framework for thematic investment hypotheses,
designed to resist LLM confabulation, narrative drift, over-admission, and backtest overfitting.
Open-ended LLM discovery is wrapped in deterministic gates and a forward pre-registration protocol,
so an idea moves capital only after surviving falsifiable, dated-evidence checks and forward (not
backtested) validation. Our central finding is methodological rather than a return claim: a
disciplined, pre-registered agent \emph{rejects most of the candidates it surfaces}, with a low,
pre-committed admission rate, while every admitted position remains auditable from the ledger alone.
\end{abstract}
